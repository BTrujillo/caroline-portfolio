SCENT MACHINE

studio: perceptions, spring 2013

critic: madeline schwartzman

This mechanized model explores the spatial occupation of essence. Three scent boxes are contained within a modulated system of pulleys which are designed to open and close the boxes at positions detached from the scent. As boxes are opened from a distance, the unknown but familiar scents diffuse through the modulated systems and gradually reveal themselves. The model takes account of essence as an integral part of the identity of a space, specifically applying to homes. The density of structure existing between the box and the place of activation represents the complex process of memory recall that takes place as one experiences a familiar scent within a space.
